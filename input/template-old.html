A filled-in box means the the episode draws events directly from the chapter (though this doesn't necessarily mean it covers the entire chapter)

A dotted box means the episode draws indirectly from the chapter, such as details of an event, revealing plot points, introducing characters, etc. - but does not directly depict events in the chapter.
